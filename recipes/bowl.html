Ground Beef Bowl

Description

This recipe is a staple of mine due to its time efficiency. It is quick and easy, and tastes amazing! This meal is a great source for protein and carbohydrates, with a customizable level of fats. While it is labelled Ground Beef Bowl, I would like to note as well that this can be made with any meat or meat substitute and will taste just as good, beef is just my personal preference!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces ground beef
  • Rice
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Heat skillet
  4. Add carrots to skillet, let cook for 3 minutes
  5. Put beef, celery, and bell peppers in skillet
  6. Season beef while cooking
  7. Once rice is ready, measure out rice
  8. Add beef
  9. Enjoy! Add any condiments you want to it.
