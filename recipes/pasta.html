Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a staple in my diet. It does not take much time to make, ingredients are straightforward, and it's a pretty simple cooking process which makes it perfect for me!

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces chicken
  • 4 ounces of fettucini
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Set pot of water to boil
  2. Cut chicken into small pieces
  3. Cut broccoli
  4. Heat skillet
  5. Add pasta to boiling water
  6. Add chicken to skillet, season while cooking
  7. Add broccoli to skillet when chicken is cooked
  8. Strain pasta
  9. Add pasta to skillet
  10. Add alfredo sauce
  11. Plate the pasta, top with parmesan cheese
  12. Enjoy!
